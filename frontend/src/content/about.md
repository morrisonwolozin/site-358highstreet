# About This Project

358 High Street is a two-unit residential duplex in Belfast, Maine, built in 2004. It was purchased in late 2023 and underwent a comprehensive deep energy retrofit completed in 2025. The project was owner-developed by Robert Morrison, P.E. of October Engineering LLC, with the goal of creating healthy, energy-efficient rental housing while fully electrifying the building.

The retrofit was designed and built by a team of Maine-based contractors and consultants, listed below.
